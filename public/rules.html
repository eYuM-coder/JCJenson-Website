Server Rules

Purpose

It is our mission to make JCJenson a safe and welcoming place for all. Every day, hundreds of people from around the world come together and express themselves on JCJenson. It is our goal to ensure all users have a valuable, inclusive and welcoming experience. These rules outline what is and isn't allowed on our Discord Server.

Preface

I - All rules in this server apply at all times and in all places. Most of the time, broken rules result in a warning - these expire with time and add up to punishments such as a mute or ban. Certain rules that are broken may come with an immediate ban.

II - Propagating or influencing those that have broken the rules may result in punishment. This includes attempting to bait people into admitting they're under 13, trying to make people spam ping you through a nickname, or quoting/replying to rule-breaking messages.

III - Not all rules in the server are listed on this page. Read the channel topic and pins of each channel to view any channel-specific rules that may be present. If you break rules in our Events server, you may also be punished in both servers. Please use common sense and avoid finding "loopholes" in the rules, they are not exhaustive and may apply to more circumstances beyond what is listed here.

IV - For moderation purposes, this server is English only. This means you cannot use ASCII, binary, morse code, ciphered code, or made-up languages from games/movies.

V - Plagiarism of any part of JCJenson include the rules, icons, or banners is not allowed and will result in a ban. In addition, please avoid distributing copyrighted content in the server such as full movies or games.

VI - We may record voice channels or streams in the server to be used privately as evidence in the event something occurs that is against the rules, and by using our VCs you give consent to this occurring.

We must also uphole Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

Rules

1. Swearing is allowed, but you are not allowed to swear excessively or use an unnecessary amount of swearing against others in a harmful way.
- You are not allowed to bypass our filter against racial slurs. "Bypassing" means changing the characters of a filtered word so the automod cannot detect what you have said. For example, incomplete slurs like n***** or f****t are not allowed.
- Directing attention to slurs in other locations (e.g file names or stickers) are also not allowed and may likely result in a warning.
2. Gore art is allowed only in our designated #gore-art channel, if you decide to post it anywhere else, mark it as a spoiler with a gore warning. Failure to do so will result in a warning unless this was forgotten by mistake.
3. Do not post malicious links or files that could be or are used to steal accounts or information anywhere, this includes surveys and "free" in-game currency scames. For this particular reason, we automod all links in our channels, so don't attempt to bypass the filter by adding characters so the automod bot cannot detect your links.
4. NSFW is not allowed ANYWHERE in the server. This includes images such as porn, R34, and more. Suggestive, NSFW pfps, or banners are not allowed. You will be asked to change this at first, and if you don't change them, you will be kicked until you change your profile/banner.
5. To keep the server peaceful, please do not be toxic by harassing, insulting, arguing, or beginning controversial topics. This can mean:
- Discriminating against users based on their race, sexual orientation, roles in the server, age, or any other personal information.
- Disrupting users by organizing large hate trains/rallies
- Discussing controversial punishments that were given to users
- Spoiling movies or other forms of media without proper warning
- Arguing with people based on serious topics or serious discussions, if you would like to argue with someone, please do it in DMs or our designated #drama-or-arguments-chat channel, and not in the server, arguing in the server will result in a warning as it is considered chat flood and it being annoying for others trying to chat and discuss topics among each other.
6. Do not spam or cause disturbance within channels, this can mean:
- Typing lots of separate lines, large blocks of text, senseless letters/characters, or random words (also known as drytexting)
- Chaining messages (for example writing lyrics that make up a song)
- Posting the same images, emojis, or stickers multiple times (up to 15 emojis at a time)
- Reacting lots of times or using epilepsy-inducing reactions/gifs
- Using cluttery fonts or text surrounded by a bunch of markings
- Not adding loud warnings to media with very loud audio
- Creating a Discord poll that is senseless and/or has 6 or more options
7. Do not use alts maliciously, this includes using them to join giveaways and give yourself an unfair advantage or to bypass a punishmment.
8. Advertising isn't allowed anywhere except within our designated #advertisements channel and in this channel, you cannot advertise anything that has NSFW or invite-for-rewards as its premise. Advertising is considered to be for example, drawing attention to a server, product, or event for your own benefit. However, if you ask people to enter your DMs for an incentive such as nitro, we will assume you are advertising something and will result in a warning. A link is not required for this to occur, and this is punishable whether in DMs, done verbally in the server or done verbally outside of the server if the staff of that server report you to our staff team.
9. Do not bring up drama anywhere except in #drama-or-arguments-chat, if you can also, keep this in DMs if possible.
10. All offensive memes should go in #offensive-memes, if you decide to post this anywhere else, the image must be marked as a spoiler and must include an offensive warning. Failure to do so is warnable regardless.
11. No impersonations of individuals in the server with profile pictures or names will be tolerated. If the person or bot you're impersonating is not in the server, you will still be subject to punishment if users are tricked by your impersonation.
12. Your display name or nickname (depending on which is shown in chat) cannot be disruptive, this means:
- Any zalgo character used in it must be reasonably sized - wide or spammy zalgo is not allowed.
- It must have at least 2 characters.
- It cannot have too many duplicates in the server.
- Changing it back after being punished may result in more action being taken against you.
13. Don't ask or distribute any personal information without consent. Personal information includes private codes to classrooms or games like Kahoot that you don't have permission to share. Exposing private information may result in a ban whether in the server or in DMs.
14. ALL UTTP MEMBERS ARE NOT ALLOWED UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES. ALL UNDERCOVER UTTP MEMBERS WILL BE BANNED ON SIGHT.
15. You aren't allowed to minimod. This means acting as a mod in any way, for example by telling people they broke a rule or saying they will be punished. If you have a problem please ping an active staff member instead of acting as one yourself.
16. Rage baiting is not allowed, you will be instantly warned.
17. Trolling is not allowed anywhere in the server. This can mean:
- Purposefully mass pinging users for no reason
- Attempting to mess up the count in the #counting channel
- Mass pinging roles without any reason
18. Warn evasion will not be tolerated. This can mean:
- Deleting your rule breaking message
- Leaving the server to evade punishment then rejoining
19. No interfering in moderator's duties, this could mean:
- Messing with them or trolling on purpose (e.g., asking for punishments, or pinging without a good reason)
- Maliciously reporting people by providing fake/forged evidence
- Misusing or exploiting any of the bots in the server
- Misinforming users with serious questions, or being disruptive in chat by constantly answering questions while staff are already doing so, doing this will result in an immediate 1 hour mute, NO exceptions.
- Arguing with staff members in chat while they moderate instead of messaging a higher ranked staff member
- Not responding to staff or dodging their questions regarding you breaking the rules
- Assisting others in avoiding punishments after they have broken the rules

If you have any questions about the rules or would like to report someone, please DM any staff member about it.
